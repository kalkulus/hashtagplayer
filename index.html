#HashtagPlayer creates a playlist of videos from tweets with #hashtag

About

Do you know about #metalfriday or #ljubavni훾etvrtak(love Thursday in Serbian)?

People use these hashtags to tweet music videos, and there's some good music there.

The only way to hear everything is to go to Twitter search, search for a #hashtag and then go from one tweet to another and play the videos.

And that get's boring really quickly. So I made #HashtagPlayer.

It is very much a demo/proof of concept at the moment, but it was fun to do it and I actually use it.

Supports only Youtube and it is limited in a number of tweets it can get.

Still, I hope you have fun with it :)
